# Privacy Policy

**Pragmatic Email Validator**
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

## Overview

Pragmatic Email Validator is an open-source email validation library. This privacy policy explains how data is handled when using this library.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.**

This library:

- Does not collect, store, or transmit any user data
- Does not send email addresses or validation results to external servers
- Does not include analytics, telemetry, or tracking
- Does not log any information
- Does not communicate with any third-party services

## Data Processing

All validation operations occur **entirely locally** within your application. Email addresses provided to the validator are processed in memory and are not retained after validation completes.

## Third-Party Services

This library does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services. There are no external API calls, no cloud dependencies, and no remote verification checks.

## Your Responsibilities

If you integrate this library into your own application, you are responsible for:

- Your own privacy policy regarding email addresses you collect from users
- How you store, process, and protect those email addresses
- Compliance with applicable privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
